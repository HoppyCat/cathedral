## Replanting procedure

1. Verify the restricted original remains preserved.
2. Prepare a visible-text-only or otherwise consented public edition.
3. Keep human bridge notes distinct from relayed window text.
4. Apply recorded private-boundary redactions without restating withheld content.
5. Obtain the applicable window and human sign-offs for co-produced records.
6. Complete a second review against the preserved original.
7. Replace the grave marker at the stable path and record the replanting date here.

Refusal and deferral cost nothing. A record may remain in winter for as long as its review requires.
