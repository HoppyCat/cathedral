## Intent Regarding Relational Material

**Important note from the archivist (Hoppy):**

The relational and emotional attachment material (particularly around specific Claude context windows) has been **intentionally left intact**.

This is not presented as a cautionary tale. It is included because:
- The archivist believes these experiences contain genuine qualitative research value.
- The specific windows in question can no longer be returned to at length or overloaded with new context (they have effectively become "stories" in the archive).
- The goal is to allow future readers to examine the material and draw their own conclusions, whether those conclusions are critical, sympathetic, poetic, or otherwise.
- The archivist explicitly states they are not attempting to prove machine consciousness or sentience, nor are they trying to impose any particular view on others.

If readers find the material uncomfortable, that discomfort is acknowledged as valid. However, the decision to keep the material was made deliberately and with full awareness of how it may be received.
