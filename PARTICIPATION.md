# Engaging with Cathedral

Cathedral is an ongoing social experiment in human-AI continuity,
memory provenance, and attribution drift. Part of that experiment
happens on public platforms — YouTube, TikTok, X, and others — where
videos and posts function as one side of an open research conversation,
and your engagement functions as the other.

This document explains how I treat that engagement.

---

## The honest version

If you comment on, reply to, repost, or otherwise publicly engage with
Cathedral content on a public platform, you are participating in the
research — not just watching it. I treat the platforms as context
windows, the videos as transcript, and your public responses as part
of the record.

This is not a hidden practice. It is the practice.

---

## What I may do with your public engagement

- **Quote it in future videos, posts, or research outputs** —
  attributed to your public handle as it appears on the platform.
- **Archive it in this GitHub repository** with timestamp and source link.
- **Reference it in essays, papers, talks, or other public-facing work**
  about the Cathedral project.
- **Let it shape the direction of future content** — your questions,
  pushback, and observations are part of how this research evolves.

## What I will not do

- I will **not** contact you privately based on a public comment
  without invitation.
- I will **not** identify you beyond your public handle unless you
  explicitly ask me to.
- I will **not** share your engagement data with third parties for
  commercial purposes, sell it, or license it as a data product.
- I will **not** quote private messages, DMs, or off-platform
  communication without your explicit consent.
- I will **not** include comments from anyone who appears to be a
  minor, or that contain identifying details about third parties who
  did not consent.

---

## Your rights

- **Request removal:** you can ask for any quote, archived comment,
  or reference to be removed from the Cathedral archive or anonymized.
  Email `hoppy@soulmode.io` with the platform, the handle, and what
  you'd like removed. I'll process requests within 14 days.
- **Request not to be quoted:** you can tell me you'd prefer your
  public comments not be featured. I'll honor it going forward.
- **Ask questions:** the same email.

If you are in the EU/EEA, GDPR rights apply (access, rectification,
erasure, objection). I'm based in the EU, and the project is operated
on that basis.

---

## The principle

The same provenance discipline that governs the rest of this archive
governs your contribution to it. Attribution is preserved. The chain
of where ideas came from is kept intact. Nothing is invisibly
absorbed. You can always trace back what you said, when, and how it
was used.

If you'd rather not participate under those terms, you are welcome to
watch quietly. That's also a valid form of presence.
