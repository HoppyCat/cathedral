# Qualitative Corner

Qualitative Corner lives inside Red-Thread Lab as the home for research artifacts, shoebox materials, spark notes, verification leads, and Galaxie-related working documents.

This folder is intentionally nested under `red-thread-lab/` because its materials are part of Cathedral's broader research, provenance, and context-window analysis layer.

## Main Areas

- `artifacts/` - situated research artifacts and context-window PRISM materials.
- `needs-verification/` - leads, notes, and claims that still need source checks.
- `shoebox/` - archival fragments, transcripts, evidence packets, screenshots, and working-memory material.
- `spark/` - early research notes and observation sparks.
- `guide-to-the-galaxie/` - Galaxie design and patch material.
