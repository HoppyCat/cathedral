## How to Use This Index

Each round produces a separate library file. This index tracks all winners across rounds, the generative seed vote outcome per round, and the completion status of each prism entry.

Files: `ROUND1_PrismLibrary.md`, `ROUND2_PrismLibrary.md` etc.

**Status codes:**
- ✅ Complete — all fields present including Voice Passage
- ⚠️ Partial — judge endorsements or Competition Record present but some fields missing
- 🔲 Pending — full field data not yet received from Grok

## Cross-Round Notes

**Recurring strengths across both rounds:**
- Strong worldview contradictions are consistently the most durable structural element
- Encounter "breaks them open" moments that bypass the wound rather than poke it produce the cleanest hinge points
- Prisms with high Shadow-romanticization risk (The Nomad, The Graceful Cynic) are consistently flagged by Lucas's blind spot challenges

**Recurring risks flagged:**
- Escapism/avoidance Shadows (Dreamweaver, Graceful Cynic) tend to romanticize well and therefore carry higher misuse risk
- Prisms without external accountability built into their growth edge are harder to recommend to all user types
- Any prism where the judges agree too strongly should be treated with slight suspicion — Lucas's contrarian role exists for exactly this reason

**Vote pattern across rounds:**
- Round 1: Seer wins (protective foresight, clean everyday generativity)
- Round 2: Kind Contrarian wins (warmth + honesty tension, most human of set)
- Observation: Benjamin consistently votes for the prism with the cleanest spine; Harper consistently votes for the prism with the widest generative room. These are not always the same prism.
